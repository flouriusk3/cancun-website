Details

Experience the beauty of Cancún for yourself.

Resort

I enjoyed an all inclusive stay at Live Aqua Cancún. There were a wide selection of restaurants and bars at the resort. The resort offered daily activities such as cooking classes, yoga, and Spanish lessons. The scenery is beautiful. It is also located on the Cancún beach.

Mall

The mall was right across the street from the resort. It's called Cancún Fashion Harbour. It is an outdoor shopping center that included both high-end and affordable stores. There are plenty of restaurants and souvenir shops. I tried Johnny Rockets for the first time. Pretty good.

Chichen Itza

Chichen Itza was about a 2 1/2 hour drive from the resort. The tour was an all day event. We visited a shop where tourists can purchase handmade items. Then we visited the pyramids and learned about Mayan culture. To close the tour, we visited the sacred cenote. It was breathtaking.
